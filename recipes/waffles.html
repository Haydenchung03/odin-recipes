Blueberry Waffles

Blueberry Waffles with Fast Blueberry Sauce

Ingredients

  • 3 egg yolks, beaten
  • 1⅔ cups milk
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2¼ teaspoons baking powder
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ¼ cup melted butter
  • 3 egg whites, stiffly beaten
  • ⅔ cup blueberries
  • 1½ cups blueberries
  • 3 tablespoons honey
  • ½ cup orange juice
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
